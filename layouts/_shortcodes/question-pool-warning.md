:warning: **Check the date on your study materials!** 

Amateur Radio license test question pools are maintained by The National
Conference of Volunteer Examiner Coordinators (NCVEC) and are updated
periodically.

Make sure that any study materials you use are valid for the current question
pool for the test you intend to take.

Visit the [NCVEC website](https://ncvec.org/) to see the question pool
validity dates.

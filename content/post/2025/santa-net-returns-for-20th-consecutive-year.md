[Santa Net] on 3.916 MHz returns for its 20th consecutive year. Part of
[The 3916 Nets], Santa Net will kick-off on November 28th and run through
December 23rd. The net will be active Thursday through Saturday night
until December 18th at which point the net will run nightly.
<!--more-->

>Pete Thomson (KE5GGY), of The 3916 Nets, commented on The Santa Net.
>He said, "We're excited to be back on the air for another year of
>spreading holiday cheer on the ham bands."

Pre-net check-ins can be made online at http://www.cqsanta.com/.

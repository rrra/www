Red River Radio Amateurs (RRRA) are pleased to continue their
tradition of public service in the Fargo/Moorhead area by providing
aid station communications for the [2026 Fargo Marathon]({{< relref
"/calendar/2026/fargo-marathon.md" >}}) on Saturday, May 30th.

:arrow_right: Licensed amateur radio operators are needed to support the
aid stations located around the Marathon course.
<!--more-->

This is an excellent training opportunity for hams interested in
event/incident communications and directed net operations. Inexperienced
operators can be paired more experienced operators, if we have enough
volunteers.

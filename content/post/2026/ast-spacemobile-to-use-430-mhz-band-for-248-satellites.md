On April 21, the [FCC announced][ann] that AST SpaceMobile (AST & Science, LLC)
will be using the Amateur 430 MHz band for 248 satellites in its planned
Low-Earth-Orbit constellation which aims to provide global cellular
broadband communications.
<!--more-->

Center frequencies are 430.5 MHz, 432.3 MHz, 434.1 MHz, 435.9 MHz, 439.5
MHz with 50 kHz bandwidth.

If you are a North Dakota Amateur Radio Operator ***[SIGN UP NOW][s]***
to operate as W1AW/0 in North Dakota between
[March 25th and March 31st, 2026][cal].

This is one of your two chances to help other operators make
W1AW/Portable contacts with North Dakota during ARRL's [America250 Worked All
States (WAS) operating event][was] for 2026.
<!--more-->

[Two hour shifts are available][s] on all Amateur Radio bands except 60M.
Previously reserved shifts are displayed at the top of the [sign up page][s].

:warning: You must [reserve your W1AW/0 shift\(s\) online][s] for this activation.

See the [guidelines for operating W1AW/Portable][guide] (from w0nd.com) for
important information about:

* Operating Etiquette
* Goodwill
* Bands
* Logging

:bangbang: **North Dakota stations operating as W1AW/0 must e-mail their
ADIF files to w0nd1@srt.com within ten (10) days of their shift(s).**
***ARRL HQ will upload the ADIF files to LoTW.***

### Rules, Guidance, and Expectations

(From the [W1AW/0 North Dakota Sign-Up page][s])

>* Operate within the limits of your license class
>* Be courteous, kind, and a good representative of the ARRL
>* You must use logging software that can export your log to an ADIF
file; that logging software should be reasonably up to date
>* Your log should be set up under the callsign W1AW/0 and should be
stored in a separate database from your personal log, if possible
>* If you are activating multiple time slots, you can use a single log
for all the time slots
>* Hams are looking forward not only to the contact with W1AW/0, but
also to awards that rely on these contacts. Please make sure to log
accurately, submit the logs promptly, and keep a backup copy
>* Following the activation, and possibly at the close of the week's
event, you will be given instructions on how/where to submit your ADIF
log
>* Please do not activate Parks on the Air using the W1AW/0 callsign [^1]

[^1]: "W1AW/0 North Dakota Sign-Up", wsm3.org, accessed March 22 2026, https://w1aw.wsm3.org/signups/ND.

### About ARRL America250 Worked All States

ARRL America250 WAS is "a year-long Worked-All-States Operating Event
celebrating the 250th anniversary (semiquincentennial) of the signing of
the Declaration of Independence." [^2]

[^2]: "ARRL America250 Worked All States". ARRL The National Association for Amateur Radio, accessed March 22 2026, https://www.arrl.org/america250-was.
